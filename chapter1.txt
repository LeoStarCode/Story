Chapter 1 — The Static Call

Maya worked the late shift at the small-town radio station, where nothing exciting ever happened—until the night the static came.
It wasn’t the usual hiss of an empty frequency. This was rhythmic, almost like breathing. Then, through the noise, a voice: “We are here.”
She froze, hand hovering over the controls. The transmission came from Channel 8, a dead, unused band the station hadn’t touched in decades.
