Chapter 3 — The Last Broadcast

At midnight, standing by the dish, Maya tuned her receiver to Channel 8. The lights above grew brighter, descending slowly.
The voice returned: “Your signal brought us. You will guide the others.”
Her eyes widened as a beam of light enveloped her, pulling her upward. The next morning, the radio station played on its own.
On Channel 8, a familiar voice spoke in perfect rhythm: “We are here.”
